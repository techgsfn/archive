Intellectual Origins

The Astitva Series arises from a longer intellectual undertaking. Much of the work presented here has grown from more than two decades of sustained svādhyāya—disciplined study, reflection, and close engagement with foundational texts, particularly the Veda and the wider traditions of Indian philosophical thought.

Over this extended period of inquiry, reflections, working notes, and exploratory writings accumulated in the course of an ongoing effort to examine questions surrounding astitva—the nature of being and existence. The present series represents the more mature expression of those investigations.

The Svādhyāya Archive

Rather than allowing these earlier explorations to disappear behind finished publications, they have been preserved in a companion scholarly archive titled Astitva: Svādhyāya-Saṁhitā.

The archive records the gradual unfolding of ideas that eventually reach a more settled form in completed works. It is presented in the spirit of intellectual transparency and serves as a documentary record of the research process through which scholarly inquiry develops over time.

Continuing Development

Now in its second decade, the Astitva Series continues to develop its digital and editorial infrastructure while remaining faithful to the purpose with which it began: the sustained exploration of knowledge traditions that take the fundamental questions of existence as their proper subject.
