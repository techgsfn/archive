The Astitva Series Astitva is the flagship scholarly series of the Global Synergetic Foundation (GSFN), an independent interdisciplinary research and publishing initiative committed to the advancement of rigorous inquiry across diverse fields of knowledge. Founded in 2015 (ISSN 2454-602X), the series fosters substantive engagement between classical knowledge traditions and the questions animating contemporary scientific thought. The series has its origins in a longer intellectual journey. Much of the work presented here has grown from over two decades of sustained svādhyāya — a disciplined practice of study, reflection, and close dialogue with foundational texts, above all the Veda and the wider traditions of Indian philosophical thought. Through these years, reflections, working notes, and exploratory writings accumulated in the course of an ongoing attempt to grapple with questions surrounding astitva — the nature of being and existence. Rather than allowing these preliminary explorations to recede behind finished publications, they have been preserved as a companion scholarly archive titled Astitva: Svādhyāya-Saṁhitā. This archive is offered in the spirit of intellectual transparency, bearing witness to the gradual unfolding of ideas that eventually find their settled form in completed works. The Astitva Series is deliberately multidisciplinary in orientation. It welcomes research that investigates the intersections of Vedic and Indian knowledge systems with contemporary fields including philosophy, mathematics, theoretical physics, cognitive and brain sciences, complex systems, and integrative medicine. Placing these domains in conversation, the series addresses enduring questions concerning knowledge, causality, consciousness, and the constitution of reality. Publications in the series encompass research monographs, advanced scholarly studies, critical editions, annotated translations, articles, and works of digital scholarship. Each contribution is held to the standards of intellectual rigour, careful documentation, and genuine openness to dialogue across disciplines and traditions. Now in its second decade, the series continues to develop its digital and editorial infrastructure while remaining faithful to the purposes that have guided it from the outset — the sustained and serious exploration of knowledge traditions that take the fundamental questions of existence as their proper subject.
